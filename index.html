Email Marketing Strategy

Building relationships and fostering loyalty through direct communication.

Customer Segments

To maximize relevance, we divide our communication into 4 key segments, each with a specific goal.

New Clients

Those in their first 30 days. The goal is to convert them into recurring clients.

Frequent Clients

Visit every 4-8 weeks. The goal is to maintain their loyalty and make them feel special.

High-Value Clients

Invest in premium services. The goal is to offer them exclusivity and recognition.

Inactive Clients

Haven't visited in 4+ months. The goal is to reactivate their interest with an attractive offer.

List Generation

To consistently cultivate and grow the subscriber list, we will use three main methods.

  • 1. Website Form: A simple form in the website footer offering a free guide in exchange for an email.
  • 2. In-Salon Registration: An invitation at checkout to join the "VIP Club" for exclusive benefits.
  • 3. Social Media: Promotion on Instagram Stories to capture interested followers.

Campaign Strategy

Our campaign is based on the concept of **tangential topics**: providing value beyond direct sales.

Welcome Campaign

For new clients. A thank-you email on day 1 and an inspiration email on day 15 to encourage a second visit.

"AriLu's Secrets" Monthly Newsletter

For frequent and high-value clients. Sent on the first Tuesday of the month at 10 AM, with tips, trends, and success stories.

"We Miss You" Reactivation Campaign

For inactive clients. A personal email after four months of inactivity with an exclusive offer to motivate their return.

Social Media Strategy

Building a visually appealing community on the right platform.

Recommended Platform

The main platform for AriLu Color Spa should be **Instagram**, due to its visual nature and an audience that matches the salon's client profile.

It offers tools like Reels, Stories, and Shopping, creating a complete ecosystem to attract and convert followers. As a secondary platform, **Pinterest** is recommended to capture users seeking inspiration.
